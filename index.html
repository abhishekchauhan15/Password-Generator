The best password tips from the pros:


1. Do not use the same password security question and answer for multiple important accounts.

2. Use a password that has at least 16 characters, use at least one number, one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter and one special symbol.

3. Do not use the names of your families, friends or pets in your passwords.4. Do not use postcodes, house numbers, phone numbers, birthdates, ID card numbers, social security numbers, and so on in your passwords.

4. Turn on 2-step authentication whenever possible.

5. Do not store your critical passwords in the cloud.
